Update AirMotion Device

Use this to update latest firmware to the AirMotion device.

Total progress
  1. Hold the small button near the airflow adjuster while powering on to enter update mode (green LED off).
  2. Select “Update Device” and wait until the update completes.
  3. Restart the device after updating.

Update AirMotion Dongle

Use this to update latest firmware to the AirMotion dongle.

Total progress
  1. Hold the small button at the back while powering on to enter update mode (screen off).
  2. Select “Update Dongle“ and wait until the update completes.
  3. Restart the device after updating.
